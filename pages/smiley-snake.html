Smiley Snake


Technologies:

CSS Animations, Phaser.io, Javascript.


Description:

A collaborative project and a new take on the classic Snake Game. Users control a chain of smileys and earn points by eating positive e mojis and lose points by colliding with negative emojis. Featuring collision detection 2D physics.
